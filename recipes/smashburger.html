Smashburger

Description

Thin burger cooked on a griddle. The burger gets flavor from the browned bits that develop during the cooking process. Those browned bits for a crust and a really good texture.

It was made popular bu the Smashburger, hamburger chain. It's name comes from the technique of smashing the burger on the grill with a bacon press or large spatula

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Beef Seasoning
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Hamburger Sauce
  • Pickles
  • Slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Form hamburger into four loosely shaped balls.
  2. Heat Griddle to about 375 degress Fahrenheit. If you dont have a griddle, use a cast iron pan.
  3. Place the balls of meat onto a hot griddle.
  4. After placing the hamburger on the hot griddle place a piece of parchment paper over the top and then smash. You want a nice thin patty. The parchment paper keeps the meet from sticking to the spatula.
  5. Add a generous amount of seasoning.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip the burger.
  8. Add cheese immediately after flipping.
